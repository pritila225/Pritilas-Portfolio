My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Annabelle Creation


This is my favourite Horror Movie.I love Annabelle: Creation because it masterfully builds tension with eerie atmosphere and suspenseful moments.

Train To Busain

Really emotional movie.The characters are well-developed, making the stakes feel personal and impactful. It’s a thrilling rollercoaster ride that explores love, sacrifice, and survival in a unique, high-tension setting.

Tangled

Super Romantic Animation Movie.The animation is stunning, and the characters, especially Rapunzel and Flynn, are so relatable and charming. The soundtrack, with songs like "I See the Light," adds a magical touch that makes the film unforgettable.
